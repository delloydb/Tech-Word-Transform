Lesson 3: Living by Faith

Subtopics

  • Faith in Action
  • Walking in Trust
  • Faith in Difficult Times

Insights

  • Faith is more than belief—it’s a lifestyle.
  • Faith keeps us grounded during trials (1 Peter 1:7).

Fun Facts

  • Martin Luther said, “Faith is a living, daring confidence in God’s grace.”

Amazing Discoveries

  • Faith-based communities often recover faster after disasters.
