Biblical Leadership: Introduction

By Pastor John Doe

45 min 12 lessons Intermediate

Lesson Video

Lesson Summary

This foundational lesson introduces biblical leadership principles through Scripture, examining what godly leadership looks like, why it matters, and how it differs from worldly leadership models. We'll explore:

  • The biblical definition of leadership
  • Key characteristics of godly leaders
  • Examples from both Old and New Testaments
  • The servant leadership model of Jesus

Course Overview

This 12-part course explores God's vision for leadership based on Scripture. It highlights principles, character traits, and examples of faithful leaders in both Old and New Testaments, offering a countercultural model of servant leadership that transforms communities.

  1. Introduction to Biblical Leadership (Current Lesson)
  2. The Heart of a Servant Leader
  3. Moses: Leading Under Pressure
  4. David: Leadership After God's Heart
  5. Nehemiah: Visionary Leadership
  6. Jesus: The Perfect Leadership Model
  7. Paul: Multiplying Leaders
  8. Developing Spiritual Authority
  9. Leading With Wisdom
  10. Building Healthy Teams
  11. Navigating Leadership Challenges
  12. Leaving a Godly Legacy
